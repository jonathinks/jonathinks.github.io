I'm Jonathan Nylander, a Swedish technologist, educator and activist currently based in Beijing, China.

I have a degree in how to code games, but these days I focus on how technology can be used to improve society.

I have taught in schools, but after reading John Holt, Seymour Papert and Peter Gray, I decided to quit the traditional teaching profession. I believe that children are people who can be trusted to make their own decisions, thus advocating and designing for Self-Directed Education.

I believe climate change is one of the most unjust and wicked problems of our time. When living in places that allow for it, I try my best to shed light on the issue.
